About

Why Layer 2 Solutions?

Ethereum mainnet faces scalability challenges — high gas fees and limited throughput make it expensive for everyday users. Layer 2 solutions like Arbitrum process transactions off the main chain while inheriting Ethereum's security, enabling thousands of transactions per second at a fraction of the cost.

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is an Optimistic Rollup that bundles multiple transactions together and submits them to Ethereum as a single batch. It assumes transactions are valid by default (optimistic) and only runs fraud proofs if someone challenges them. This approach dramatically improves scalability while preserving Ethereum-level security.

Real-World Benefit

A simple token swap that costs $50+ on Ethereum mainnet can cost less than $0.50 on Arbitrum — a massive reduction in fees while maintaining strong security guarantees. DeFi protocols and NFT applications can finally serve mainstream users affordably.
